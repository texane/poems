\section{\dotfill Made Of Ink}
\large

\resetcolorseries[16]{verso}
\begin{verso}
\makebox[.6em][c]{\lettrine{H}{}}ere, what's not white
Is filled with black.
No striking light
No fading black.
\end{verso}

\begin{verso}
There is no joy
In what I think.
'Cause I'm a boy
All made of ink.
\end{verso}

\hfill{FLM, 17 December 2014}

\section{\dotfill Interia}
\large

\resetcolorseries[24]{verso}
\begin{verso}
\makebox[1em][c]{\lettrine{T}{}}here is no shame in getting lost
What is foolish, is staying frost.
Waiting for things, 'till it's too late,
So find your path, fulfill your fate.
\end{verso}

\hfill{FLM, 1 January 2015}
